Traditional Dress

Men wear Gamsa ,Aronai and women wear dkhna,jwmgra and Aronai. These traditional dresses are an important part of Bodo identity.

Festivals

Assam celebrates colorful festivals called Domashi. A january harvest festival marked by bonfires and communal feasts.

Religion

Bathouism is the main religion in Bodo people and A sijou plant is traditionally planted in the courtyard to represent the altar of the supreme god.
